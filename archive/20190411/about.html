ABOUT SUNSPOT

Fresh, fantastic food, crafted with passion and served in an easygoing atmosphere. Consistently voted Best Vegetarian, Best Brunch, Best Lunch Spot and Best Wine List in Knoxville by Metro Pulse readers, Sunspot caters to both vegetarians and carnivores with a unique, multicultural blend of Southwestern, Caribbean and Latin American cuisines.

Conveniently located on Cumberland Avenue in Knoxville, Tennessee — Sunspot reminds you that you don't have to be in college to enjoy “The Strip.” We offer over 40 beers on tap as well as a selection of wines and spirits fit to enjoy in good company at our smoke-free bar or outdoors on our rooftop patio amongst fellow weather admirers.

A comfortable place to relax, with your friends, Sunspot: where tie dyes and neckties unite.
