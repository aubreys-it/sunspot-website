About Sunspot Restaurant

Sunspot: food made with passion served in an easy‑going atmosphere. Consistently voted in the Best Vegetarian, Best Brunch, Best Lunch Spot, Best Wine List categories by Metropulse readers, Sunspot caters to vegetarians and carnivores with with a unique and multicultural blend of Southwestern, Caribbean and Latin American cuisines. Our diverse menu offers tasty items such Jerk Spiced Tuna Salad, Buffalo Fried Tofu, Roasted Pork Burrito or our Solar Flare Veggie Burger and all of our dishes begin with our famous tomatillo salsa and fresh tortilla chips.

Conveniently located on Cumberland Avenue in Knoxville, Tennessee — Sunspot reminds you that you don't have to be in college to enjoy "The Strip." We offer an extensive selection of draft beers, wines and spirits all to be enjoyed in our cozy smoke-free bar.

We are open until 2:00 am Tuesday through Saturday and until midnight on Sunday and Monday. A late night menu is served in the bar nightly. A comfortable place to relax with your friends, Sunspot: where tie dyes and neckties unite.
